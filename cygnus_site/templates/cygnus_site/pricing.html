Flat Rate Pricing:

Our fee for accounting service is based upon the volume of transactions and also on the overall complexity of your business. Factors affecting our fee structure include:

  • Number of customer and vendors invoices

  • Number of checks written per period

  • Number of employees and pay frequency

  • Financial and management reports requirement

  • Number of bank accounts and credit card accounts to be reconciled

  • Inventory

  • Current state of your accounting records

  • other misc. items
